Aloha, Hawai'i

[A L O H A]
This Hawaiian word is used when greeting or parting from someone. There is a deeper meaning to the word Aloha and it is often described as "the spirit of aloha"


Let's see some ways this is used:
  1. The Aloha Spirit
  2. The Way of Aloha

But what does ALOHA really mean?
It can be broken down by letters or by phrases. Growing up children in Hawai'i often learn the "fundamental code of ethics" which is often found in the word Aloha.
  • A, ala, watchful, alterness
  • L, lokahi, working with unity
  • O, oia'i'o, truthful honesty
  • H, ha'aha'a, humility
  • A, ahonui, patient perseverance
